Our Story

Meet the Bride & the Groom

Alaina & Kenny

Bride & Groom
Alaina and Kenny met in November 2019 on OkCupid. Their first date was at Island Delight in downtown Montgomery. In March 2020, Kenny asked Alaina to be his girlfriend. Between work and school schedules during a pandemic, the two still made time for one another. They attend church every week, work out regularly, and find creative things to do roughly once a month on what they call “big date night.”
For "big date night," the couple alternates who plans each month and generally keeps the details a surprise. On their first big date night, they got dressed up and had a nice take-out dinner at the dining room table followed by salsa lessons on YouTube. They’ve also been ziplining, hiking, and axe throwing. They've taken a day trip to the beach and painted King and Queen canvases in the garage.
On their first big date night of 2021 (January 29th to be exact), Kenny asked Alaina to wear after-five attire and drove them to Central in downtown Montgomery. The waitress asked if they were celebrating anything special, to which Kenny responded “No” but was thinking about saying “Yes.” At the end of their dinner, Kenny stated that that was all he had planned for the night. As he walked Alaina to the car, Kenny told her that he had finally come up with his word of the year. This is what the couple does in lieu of new year’s resolutions. His word?
Promotion. So, he got down on one knee and asked Alaina if she would accept the promotion to be his wife.

FAQs

Frequently Asked Questions

What should I wear? Is there a dress code?

After 5 attire is preferred.

Will the ceremony and reception be indoors or outdoors?

The ceremony will take place outdoors. The reception will take place indoors. Please check the weather forecast for Prattville, Alabama so that you are properly dressed.

What time should I arrive?

We recommend that you arrive an hour before the start of the ceremony to make sure everyone is on time and we can get the party started as scheduled!

Are kids welcome?

While we love your little ones, our wedding is going to be an adults-only event (21+) so that everyone can relax and enjoy the evening. We appreciate the time that you will spend celebrating with us.

Do you have a hotel block for guests? Where do you recommend I stay?

Hotel information will be provided at a later time.

When is the RSVP deadline? Do I need to mail back my RSVP or can I submit it online?

You can mail your RSVP back. However, to ensure that it is received in a timely manner, we recommend that you submit your RSVP online.

Will food and drinks be served at the reception? What kind of food?

Yes, food will be served. The type of food will be announced at a later time.

Will there be any vegan, vegetarian, or special diet options?

Yes, there will. We understand that everyone doesn't eat meat for their various reasons. Please select your dietary choice when submitting your RSVP.

May I take a centerpiece, candle, or flowers home as a souvenir?

Yes. The centerpieces were made as wedding favors. Feel free to take them. We ask that you only take one so that there are enough to go around.

Can I take and post pictures of the wedding on social media?

Yes. We encourage you to take and post pictures on social media using the hashtag #KalainyEverAfter so that we can keep track of them. However, we ask that you refrain from taking pictures during the ceremony.

I have a question not answered above. What is the best way to contact you?

Send a text or email to the bride or groom.
