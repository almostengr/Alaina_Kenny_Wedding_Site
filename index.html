Our Story

Meet the Bride & the Groom

Alaina & Kenny

Bride & Groom
Alaina and Kenny met in November 2019 on OkCupid. Their first date was at Island Delight in downtown Montgomery. In March 2020, Kenny asked Alaina to be his girlfriend. Between work and school schedules during a pandemic, the two still made time for one another. They attend church every week, work out regularly, and find creative things to do roughly once a month on what they call “big date night.”
"Big date night" alternates who plans each month and generally keep the details a surprise. On their first big date night, they got dressed up and had a nice take out dinner at the dining room table followed by salsa lessons on YouTube. They’ve also been ziplining, hiking, gone axe throwing, taken a day trip to the beach, and painted King and Queen canvases in the garage.
On their first big date night of 2021 (January 29th to be exact), Kenny asked Alaina to wear After 5 attire and drove them to Central in downtown Montgomery. The waitress asked if they were celebrating anything special, to which Kenny responded “No” but was thinking about saying “yes”. At the end of their dinner, Kenny stated that that was all he had planned for the night. As he walked Alaina to the car, Kenny told her that he had finally come up with his word of the year. This is what the couple does in lieu of new year’s resolutions. His word? Promotion. So, he got down on one knee and asked Alaina if she would accpet the promotion to be his wife.
